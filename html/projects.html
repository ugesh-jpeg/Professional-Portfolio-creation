My Projects

Redesign of Amazon's Mobile App

Objective: Improve the user experience of Amazon's mobile shopping app to enhance usability, increase purchase rates, and reduce cart abandonment.

Scope:

  • Conducted extensive user research to identify usability issues.
  • Developed wireframes and prototypes, focusing on a simplified navigation structure and more intuitive product discovery.
  • Collaborated closely with Amazon's development team to ensure design feasibility.
  • Led A/B testing efforts to measure the impact of design changes on user engagement and conversion rates.
  • Presented design solutions to Amazon’s stakeholders, highlighting projected improvements in key performance metrics.

Outcome: Achieved a 25% increase in purchase rates and a 20% reduction in cart abandonment, with significant positive feedback from users.

Omnichannel Experience for Nike

Objective: Design a seamless omnichannel experience for Nike, integrating online and in-store shopping to create a cohesive brand experience.

Scope:

  • Collaborated with cross-functional teams to map customer journeys across digital and physical channels.
  • Introduced features such as real-time inventory updates, personalized product recommendations, and in-store pickup options.
  • Conducted ethnographic studies to understand customer behavior and preferences.
  • Developed high-fidelity prototypes and led user testing sessions to validate design concepts.
  • Worked closely with Nike's development team to implement designs and iterate based on user feedback.

Outcome: Launched a successful omnichannel experience that increased customer satisfaction by 35% and drove a 30% increase in repeat purchases.

AI-Powered Personalization for Netflix

Objective: Design an AI-driven personalization engine for Netflix to enhance content recommendations based on user preferences and viewing behavior.

Scope:

  • Led the UX/UI design process, focusing on creating a personalized and engaging user experience.
  • Collaborated with Netflix's data science team to develop and integrate AI algorithms into the recommendation engine.
  • Implemented adaptive interfaces that respond dynamically to user interactions and preferences.
  • Facilitated design workshops to align project goals with business objectives and user needs.
  • Created a comprehensive design system to ensure consistency across all user interfaces.

Outcome: Increased user engagement by 50% and improved retention rates by 25%, positioning Netflix as a leader in personalized streaming experiences.

Global Design System for Google

Objective: Develop and implement a global design system for Google, unifying design standards across all products and platforms to enhance user experience and brand consistency.

Scope:

  • Led a distributed team of designers and developers to create a comprehensive design system used across Google’s products.
  • Drove UX strategy to ensure alignment with Google’s long-term vision and product goals.
  • Conducted global user research and workshops to gather insights and ensure the system's flexibility and scalability.
  • Mentored junior designers and teams in adopting and utilizing the design system effectively.
  • Published a white paper on the design system's impact and best practices for implementation.

Outcome: Successfully implemented the design system, reducing design and development time by 50% and significantly enhancing user experience across Google’s products.
